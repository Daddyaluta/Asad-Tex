About ASAD TEXTILES.NG

Your trusted partner in quality textiles since 2020

Our Story

Founded in 2020, ASAD TEXTILES.NG began as a small fabric store in Lagos with a vision to provide high-quality textiles to fashion designers, tailors, and home decorators across Nigeria.

Over the years, we've grown into one of Nigeria's leading textile distributors, known for our exceptional quality, competitive pricing, and outstanding customer service.

Today, we supply fabrics to over 500 businesses across Nigeria and continue to expand our product range to meet the evolving needs of our customers.
